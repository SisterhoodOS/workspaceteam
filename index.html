Basis of calculation

Which Version This Follows

This workspace follows the document signed on 16 July 2026, the version stating admin hours of 15 to 25 hours per week.

That works out to roughly 65 to 108 hours a month, or IDR 2,600,000 to 4,330,000 per month in admin. Hours above 25 per week require Sophia's written approval in advance.

Note: a separate PDF is in circulation stating 15 to 25 hours per month. That version is not used here. Every other rate is identical across both, so nothing else changes.
Payday differs from the contract wording. Section 8 of the signed document says payment lands by the 5th of the following month. In practice Monika is paid on the 2nd, so every due date in this workspace is calculated from the 2nd.

Worth confirming with Sophia so the document and the actual practice agree.

General Terms

TermDetail
Hours trackingWeekly itemized log shared with Sophia. Every entry states what was done, not just the hours. Untracked or undescribed hours are not billable.
ApprovalProject work (batches, builds, production days) is commissioned in writing before it begins.
PaymentOne monthly invoice combining hours and deliverables, paid by the 5th of the following month.
Rate reviewQuarterly, or if IDR to USD moves more than 10% sustained over 60 days.
ScopeNo people management. Client-facing sends and posts require Sophia's approval.
Build scope ruleA build is quoted and agreed in writing before work starts. Copywriting, brand design, and strategy are not included. Time spent learning a new platform or tool is included in the fixed project rate and is never billed as hours.
No double billingProduction days are not billed hourly on top. Meetings inside logged admin hours are not billed separately.

Important

Where Your Data Lives

Read this one. Your data is saved inside the browser on this computer only. It is never sent to the internet, and nobody else can see it, Sophia included.

That means: open this page on your phone or another computer and it will be empty. Clear your browser history and it is gone.

So at the end of every month, press Download Backup below and keep the file. That is your safety net. The backup holds your work log, daily tasks, commitments, salary records, the petty cash ledger, and every attached image.
